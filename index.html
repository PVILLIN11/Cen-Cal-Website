Professional Engineering Services

Cen Cal Engineering provides comprehensive engineering and construction services throughout Central California. From project management to underground utilities, we deliver quality solutions for residential and commercial projects.

About Cen Cal Engineering

Based in Tulare, California, Cen Cal Engineering specializes in general engineering, project management, general building, ADUs, JADUs, construction services. Our experienced team handles everything from underground utilities, septic systems, grading, earthwork, and construction staking.

We are certified by the California ADA and Traffic Safety/Flagger programs, ensuring our projects meet the highest safety and accessibility standards. Whether you need civil inspections, base flood elevation certificates, or complete project management, we bring professional expertise to every job.
